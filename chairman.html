Message From The Chairman

Dr. Siraz Meerasahib, (Ph.D)

Chairman Metropolitan College

At Metropolitan College we believe that good education transforms the mind. Our mission is to make our students intellectually competent, morally strong, socially committed and spiritually inspired so that they can contribute towards nation building. Our prime objective is to equip them with right values, attitudes, beliefs and capabilities to face the ever-changing world.

We have created state-of-the-art academic infrastructure, putting together the best of teaching talent, equipping with every required learning aid to ensure that students at Metropolitan College get the best in terms of education, facilities and faculty. Our commitment towards excellence helps us to set outstanding teaching standards. At Metropolitan College, we build every student into a competent and successful individual.

To provide our students with a total international exposure we are closely tied up with the largest American style university in Europe, the Girne American University, OTHM-UK. Through these collaborations we stand tall as the provider of widest range of international programmes in the country with 40 other Metropolitan programmes registered under Tertiary and Vocational Education Commission of Sri Lanka. Further, I assure my prospective students that Metropolitan College will work towards providing more international exposure and continue to sustain as the best education facilitator in Sri Lanka for local as well as international students.

Thus, I take pleasure in welcoming all ambitious students, aspiring to excel in the years ahead, to join and follow our prestigious programmes.
